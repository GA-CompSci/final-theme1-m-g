About Me And My Business

I am a 15-year-old freshman who works hard in school, on the field, and especially for my business. I run my own car detailing business and take pride in making cars look their best. I play football and basketball, and both sports have taught me discipline, teamwork, and how to stay focused, which help me for this business.

When I detail cars, I take small steps to make the cars look amazing. I provide A top notch wash that includes detailing, a full service wash, tire cleaning, bug removing, polishing, interior detailing, steam clean/vacuum, stain remover, and exceptional interior cleaning.

What We Do

Interior Deep Clean

We get into every nook and cranny. From vacuuming and salt removal to steam cleaning upholstery and wiping down every surface, we make your cabin feel brand new again.

Precision Exterior Detailing

We use a multi-stage, hand-wash process designed to safely lift away road grime and contaminants. By using professional grade tools and techniques, we ensure a deep clean that protects your paint's integrity and leaves a streak-free shine.

Full Detail Express

The ultimate refresh for your daily driver. This package combines our interior and exterior services to ensure your vehicle is clean, sanitized, and looking its best from the driveway to the highway.
